## ✅ Summary

**What you'll see when you run the app:**

1. **Black/White Theme** - Clean, modern, professional
2. **Login Screen** - 8 login options (email, Google, Facebook, SMS OTP, WhatsApp OTP, etc.)
3. **Register Screen** - Complete form with country codes and 3 verification methods
4. **Forgot Password** - Simple email-based reset
5. **OTP Screens** - Both SMS and WhatsApp OTP support

**Everything matches your website perfectly!** 🎉
